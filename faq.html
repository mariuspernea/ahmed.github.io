Frequently Asked Questions


How and what do I need to do to make an appointment?

You can call the hospital to book an appointment, have in hand your insurance details, who referred you (or if it is a self-referral). Bring whatever relevant documents (X-rays, MRIs, CT scans) and a list of your medications.


How long is the consultation visit?

Generally, a new consultation visit is around 30 minutes. However we will take all time needed if necessary.


How and what do I need to do to make an appointment?

We have several practice locations as detailed on this website. Please have a look at the Hospitals or Contact section.


What do I do the day of surgery?

In the days before your scheduled procedure, the hospital will contact you and give you the information you need for the day of surgery.


What if I do not have insurance?

You can pay for your consultation, follow-ups and surgery (if needed) by cash or cheque if you are not insured.
